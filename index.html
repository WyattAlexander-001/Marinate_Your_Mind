Marinating the Material

Welcome dear viewer, this is a simple single page website for the purpose of sharing my philosophy on studying. I dub my technique, “marinating the material.” I also share how I personally feel about other studying techniques. The term “marination” is a direct reference to one of my favorite meals, a nice marinated steak because all good things take thyme!

The core idea is that you want to have your mind constantly surrounded, or “marinated in” with the material you want to learn...
